About Us

What is the T.I.P.I.D. Club?

A school-based organization dedicated to helping students develop financial literacy, responsible money habits, and practical life skills.

Introduction

T.I.P.I.D. Club is a school-based organization dedicated to helping students develop financial literacy, responsible money habits, and practical life skills. Through interactive activities and peer support, the club teaches students how to save, budget, and make smart financial decisions that will benefit them in the future.

Why Did We Create the Club?

The club was created after observing that many students struggle with managing their allowance and making responsible spending decisions. T.I.P.I.D. Club provides a supportive environment where students can learn practical financial skills and build habits that promote financial independence and discipline.

Our Mission

To empower students with financial knowledge, practical skills, and disciplined habits that promote responsible money management.

Our Vision

To develop financially responsible, disciplined, and future-ready students who can make wise financial decisions and contribute positively to society.
